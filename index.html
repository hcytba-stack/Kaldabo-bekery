The Story Behind ቃል ዳቦ

ቃል (Qal) means a Word or Promise. We bake to fulfill our promise of delivering fresh, healthy, standard premium baked products daily.

Our Process

Baked with Love, Authenticity, & Ethiopian Heart

Started in the heart of Addis Ababa, ቃል ዳቦ Bakery set out to make artisan baking accessible to every household via swift logistics. We bake traditional delicacies including Ethiopian Difo bread inside simulated wood-fired styled ovens utilizing traditional herbs such as banana leaves, ginger, and cardamom, and modern cakes using imported top-shelf chocolate and fresh cream.

Our premium distribution allows customers to make seamless choices for family feasts, weddings, holidays, and birthday surprises, with a robust automated ticket tracking delivery system.
